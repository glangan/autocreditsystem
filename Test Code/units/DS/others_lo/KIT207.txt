On successful completion of this unit, you will be able to:
Students should be ICT professionals with the abilities and skills to:
Apply agile development methodologies to the design and production of games and virtual worlds;
Understand the relationship between the game designer and other members of the development team, including the ability to develop and modify art assets and scripts
Design games and virtual worlds that appeal to a target audience and implement prototypes to inform and evaluate their design.
Students should acquire attitudes needed by an ICT professional to:
be an effective team member;
apply a user-centred approach when designing a game/virtual world;
take initiative and work independently;
communicate effectively at a professional level;
use abstraction and computational, creative and critical thinking to problem solve;
continue lifelong learning;
act in accordance with best practice and industry standards.
